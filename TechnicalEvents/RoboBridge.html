Robo Bridge


About The Event

Robobridge is a robotic event in which the robot has to carry particular objects using the arm mechanism to the given destination.

Objective

To design a wired/wireless manually controlled robot having the ability to lift the objects from one place to another using arm mechanism. This task should be performed on an arena which will be designed like bridge.

Bot Specifications

  • Bot dimension should be such that it can be enclose inside a box of dimensions 30cm*30cm*30cm.
  • The weight of the bot should not exceed 3 kg.
  • The bot can be wired or wireless manually controlled.
  • Battery voltage should not be more than 12 volts.
  • The bot must have on-board power supply.
  • The bot should be controlled using a single wired remote in case of wired and by a single device or wireless remote in case of wireless bot.

Team Specification

  • The maximum number of members in a team should not exceed 4.
  • Student from different colleges can be a part of single team.
  • An individual cannot be a part of more than one team.

Competition Rules

The event will be conducted in two phases.

  • Transporter
  • Ascender

    Transporter

  • Transporter is a qualifier round in which the bot has to travel on a plane surfaced bridge with the object (size and shape may vary) in the arm and transport it to another location.
  • The shipment of the objects will carry a constant point and there will be negative markings in different situations.
  • The bot should not deal any damage to the arena. If there is any kind of damage to the arena than the negative marks will be given to the team.

    • Ascender

  • Ascender is the final round of robobridge in which the bot has to climb the bridge of different elevations with the object in the arm.
  • There will be specified location for different colored or shaped objects in which it has to be placed by the robot.
  • Touching the bot in the middle of the task will result in penalty.
