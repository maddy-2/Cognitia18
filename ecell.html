ABOUT THE EVENT

1.Adhole:

  • Participants will make a team of two people .
  • They will be given a product(name) and have to write an advertisement based on the given product .
  • Judgement will be on basis of content.

2. Trading Strategies:

  • Trading Strategies includes tactics of buying and selling the prouduct after the product have been launched.
  • Participants will make a team of two people .
  • And it will have three rounds-
  • Round1: Quiz
  • Quiz will be based on general business and management . Selected team after this round will go for next two rounds.
  • Round2:Product Launch
  • Each team will be provided with any random product and they have to launch the product.
  • Judgement will be on the basis how they present their ideas and motives behind launching the product.
  • Round3: Product sell/Best seller
  • Each team have to sell the product that they have launched in round 2.
  • They have to put their ideas regarding the quality ,price,features of the product. They have to sell the product in convenient way.

3. Best Manager:

  • Individual participant will be provided with a problem statement related to some particular situation that may arise in an organisation and they to give solution for the problem and how they handle and proceed in that situation.
