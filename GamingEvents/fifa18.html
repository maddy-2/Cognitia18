FIFA 18


About

FIFA 18 is a sports game that simulates association football. The game features 52 fully licensed stadiums from 12 countries, including new stadiums, plus 30 generic fields for a total of 82. All 20 Premier League stadium are represented in the series.

General Rules

  • Each teams or players must bring their own gaming gear such as mouse, keyboard, headphones/earphones and joystick.
  • Toxicity in game or outside the game will not be tolerated.
  • No bags are allowed inside the rooms where the games are being played. Only the gaming gear are allowed.
  • No one is allowed in the room where the games are being played if he/she is not a participant that is going to play or he/she is not part of the event team.
  • All the teams and players must be on time or else it will be a direct disqualification.
  • Indiscipline actions will not be tolerated.

Specfic Rules

  • The matches will be played 1v1.
  • All matches will be a best of one (bo1) match.
