Shimmer


ABOUT THE EVENT

  • Shimmer is the annual freshers organised for the 1st yearites. It is focused just to highlight the talent and capabilities of the 1st yearites. It is composed of 4 rounds:
  • Round 1: The freshers are made to write an evaluation test passing which they go to Round 2.
  • Round 2: This is a group discussion round which is organised to test the communication skills of the participants.
  • Round 3: This is a personal interview round where they are tested for their ideologies in various ethical and practical fields to see if they are fit for the title of Mr or Miss Shimmer.
  • Fourth and final round: They are made to perform during the night of Cognitia where they'll be judged by a panel of faculties based on their talents and a question & answer round followed by a Ramp walk.
