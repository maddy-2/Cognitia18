Invento


Description

  • Participants will be asked to prepare an innovative mini-project based on electrical engg. theme. The materials used should be as cheap (of no use) as possible, a detailed explanation can be provided by the respective co-ordinators.
  • Participants will be selected for the next round on the basis of how efficient their projects are. (to be decided on the basis of: [(degree of innovativeness) divided by(expenditure)]
  • In this round Participants have to propose on-spot verbal solutions to asked “Real world problems related to electrical engineering” ;they will be scored as per their communication as well as convincing skills irrespective of language used(English/hindi).
  • The average of the scores of these two rounds will decide the winners.
