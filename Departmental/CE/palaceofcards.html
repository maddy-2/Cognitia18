Palace Of Cards


ABOUT:

A fun event that lifts up the heights of card arrangement and concentric analysis, requiring great patience and technique.

DESCRIPTION:

This event holds the fun of raising the card triangles over constrains of time and cards.

COMPETITION RULES

  1. In teams of 2 member each.
